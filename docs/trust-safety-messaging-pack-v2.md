## 1. User FAQ (5)

**1) What is Aura?**  
Aura is a **calm safety companion on the web** that helps you share journey updates with people you trust, check in when you want, and reach emergency-style help in one or two taps when you feel stressed or unsafe. It is built for **clarity under pressure**, not scary alerts.

**2) Does Aura replace 911, police, or a doctor / therapist?**  
**No.** Aura is a **tool to coordinate with people you choose** and to surface help paths you already have. In an emergency, use the emergency number or service **where you live**. Aura does not provide medical or legal advice and is not a substitute for professional care.

**3) Who can see my location or when I use SOS?**  
You decide **who is in your trusted circle** and when sharing is on. Location and SOS-related actions are explained **before you confirm** sensitive steps, consistent with our onboarding and settings copy. Exact behavior depends on your setup (for example, whether a live account and backend are connected); see in-app explanations and [`docs/PUBLIC_BETA.md`](./PUBLIC_BETA.md) for beta scope.

**4) Is Aura “watching” me all the time?**  
**No always-on surveillance story.** Aura uses permissions like location **when you use features that need them**, with primers ahead of browser prompts where applicable. Default framing is **local-first until you connect a live account** — see trust spec above.

**5) What if something goes wrong in the app?**  
If the app can’t sync or something fails, we aim to describe it as a **session or connection issue**, not as a comment on your personal safety. That keeps stress lower and matches our UX safety baseline.

## 2. Partner blurb (orgs / communities, ~160 words)

Aura is a calm, mobile-friendly **web companion** for people who want to stay connected to **trusted contacts** and keep **clear, low-friction paths to help** when stress runs high — for example when traveling, commuting late, or navigating unfamiliar situations. It is designed to complement, not replace, your existing **wellness, EAP, crisis, and emergency resources**: we do not diagnose, predict outcomes, or guarantee that any alert will reach a specific person or service. Organizations and communities can offer Aura as an **optional layer** members adopt at their own pace: journey check-ins, transparent sharing choices, and SOS-style flows that emphasize **confirm before anything is sent**. We lead with **consent, plain language, and privacy-respecting defaults** (see our onboarding and settings documentation), and we avoid fear-based marketing. For pilots, we provide beta runbooks and observability notes so partners can set expectations honestly. If Aura fits your duty-of-care story, position it as **one supportive tool** alongside professional and emergency options — aligned with our public narrative and partner one-pager.

## 3. Responsible marketing guardrails (checklist)

| Avoid claiming… | Say instead… |
| --- | --- |
| Aura **prevents** harm, assault, stalking, or crime | Aura **supports planning, check-ins, and reaching help** you choose; outcomes depend on many factors outside the product |
| Aura **predicts** danger or “knows” you are unsafe | Aura offers **structured ways to signal need for help** and share context; users stay in control of what they send |
| Aura **replaces** 911, clinicians, lawyers, or crisis lines | Aura **complements** official and professional resources; **direct people to appropriate services** for emergencies and care |
| **Guaranteed** delivery to contacts or authorities | Describe **intent and confirmations** in product; for beta, note **known limitations** from [`docs/PUBLIC_BETA.md`](./PUBLIC_BETA.md) |
| **Medical / mental-health treatment** or therapeutic outcomes | **Well-being and safety workflow** language; no therapy or diagnosis framing |
| **Always-on tracking** or surveillance | **Permission-based** use, **transparent settings**, **user-initiated** sharing |
| **Fear, shame, or graphic scenarios** to sell the product | **Calm, respectful** tone; **agency and dignity** for the audience ([`launch-narrative.md`](./launch-narrative.md)) |
| **Unverifiable stats** (“99% safer”) | **Product behaviors**, **privacy boundaries**, and **what we measure in beta** per [`docs/beta-analytics-outcomes-narrative.md`](./beta-analytics-outcomes-narrative.md) where relevant |

**Pre-publish pass:** Compare hero and body copy to [`docs/launch-narrative.md`](./launch-narrative.md) and [`docs/partner-distribution-onepager.md`](./partner-distribution-onepager.md); escalate legal/medical review if a campaign implies regulated health or emergency services.
